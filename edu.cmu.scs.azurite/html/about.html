Azurite

Adding Zest to Undoing and Restoring Improves Textual Exploration

A plug-in for Eclipse, which brings selective undo features into Eclipse.

Azurite is developed by YoungSeok Yoon as part of Natural Programming Group and Exploratory Programming Group.
